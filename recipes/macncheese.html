Macaroni and Cheese

Description

Its an italian dish

It also mostly only utilizes cheese.

Ingrediants

  • Farfalle Pasta
  • Butter
  • Shredded Cheese

Steps

  1. Start by boiling a pot of water.
  2. Then put the macaroni in the boiling water.
  3. After pasta has been boiled, drain out the water.
  4. Put some butter in the pot and let it melt.
  5. Put shreeded cheese in pasta.
  6. Stir until melted.
